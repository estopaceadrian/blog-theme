Chess

Played since 1992

Posted by Aestopace on May 28, 2022

My father taught me how to play Chess. Me and my siblings were able to learn from him. I remember that there was a time when he will ask us to play with him then if we won, he will give us PHP100. It was fun, and I didn't win that time.

As we grow older, me and my siblings, eventually beat down our schoolmates and represent the school to compete with other schools.

During college days, we would still compete as a varsity team.

I will always cherish the things that my father taught us.
